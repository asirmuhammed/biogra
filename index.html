Tovino Thomas (born 21 January 1989) is an Indian actor and film producer who prominently works in Malayalam films.[2] He made his debut on 28 January 2012 with Albert S in the Malayalam film Prabhuvinte Makkal. His breakthrough roles were in the films ABCD (2013), 7th Day (2014) and Ennu Ninte Moideen (2015). He won the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actor for his performance in Ennu Ninte Moideen (2015). He also won the Kerala Film Critics Association Award and Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actor for his performance in Mayanadi (2017). He starred as the titular character in the Netflix superhero film Minnal Murali (2021).[3] For his constantly evolving looks and style, Tovino is ranked among the most desirable men[4] from Kerala published by the Kochi Times, which is a subsidiary of The Times of India.
